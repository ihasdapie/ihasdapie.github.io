Retrospective: Internship @ Open Robotics

Date: Aug 18, 2022, Last Modified: Nov 17, 2023
Tags [ robots programming about_me ]

I spent summer 2022 interning at Open Robotics1, a non-profit organization that spawned 2 and maintains ROS, ROS2 (Robot Operating System) and the Gazebo simulator.

Since writing up an retrospective is a lot more work than I’d like to put in right now, here’s the output of gh search prs --involves ihasdapie -L 100 in the relevant ros orgs, if that’s of interest. It’s also pretty cool to see the Iron Irwini Release Notes since I worked on a number of features there, most notably service introspection
